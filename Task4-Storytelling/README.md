# DATA-STORYTELLING-STATISTICAL-VALIDATION-2025-
📊 FMCG Sales Analytics — Andhra Pradesh (2025)
🔍 Project Summary

This project presents an end-to-end analysis of FMCG sales data to uncover revenue drivers, customer behavior, retention patterns, and operational risks.

The objective was to transform raw transactional data into actionable business insights and validate findings using statistical techniques.

🎯 Business Objectives
Analyze overall sales performance and growth trends
Identify key revenue-driving categories and customers
Evaluate customer retention and lifecycle behavior
Detect business risks such as dependency and seasonality
Provide data-driven recommendations for growth
📁 Dataset Overview
📊 90,866 transaction records
💰 ₹9.54 Crore total revenue
👥 1,219 active customers
🛒 19 FMCG product categories
📅 Time period: January – December 2025
📌 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
KPI	Value	Business Insight
Total Revenue	₹9.54 Cr	Overall business scale
Avg Order Value	₹6,866	Customer spending behavior
Active Customers	1,219	Customer base size
Top Category	Cigarettes (~37%)	Revenue concentration
Credit Share	~69%	High financial exposure
📈 Key Insights
🔹 Revenue Analysis
Cigarettes contribute ~37% of total revenue
Top 3 categories contribute ~75% of overall revenue
Strong seasonal trend with peak in December (~₹1.2 Cr)
Revenue nearly doubles from March to December
🔹 Customer Analysis
278 Champion customers generate a major share of revenue
High dependency on a small customer base
Risk of revenue loss if key customers churn
🔹 Retention Analysis
January cohort shows strong retention (~88%)
March and May cohorts show weak retention (~28%)
Indicates inconsistency in customer acquisition quality
🔹 Operational Insights
~69% revenue comes from credit transactions
Discount rate is extremely low (~0.12%)
Data quality is high and financially consistent

🔹 Hypothesis Testing

Statistical validation of key insights:

Hypothesis	Test	Result	Business Impact
