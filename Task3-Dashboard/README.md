# 📊 FMCG Sales Analysis — Andhra Pradesh (2025)

## 🚀 Project Overview

This project delivers a **deep-dive analysis and interactive dashboard** for FMCG sales data across Andhra Pradesh for the year 2025.
The objective is to transform raw transactional data into **actionable business insights** using analytics, segmentation, and visualization.

---

## 📌 Key Business Highlights

* 💰 **Total Revenue:** ₹8.32 Cr (Invoiced) 
* 👥 **Active Customers:** 1,219
* 🧾 **Total Invoices:** 12,125
* 📦 **Average Order Value (AOV):** ₹6,866
* 🚬 **Top Category:** Cigarette (37.3% of total revenue)
* 📅 **Best Month:** December (₹1.08 Cr revenue peak)
* 💳 **Credit Sales:** 69.1% (₹5.75 Cr → high dependency)
* 🏆 **Champion Customers:** 278 (highest revenue contributors)

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Define and track **core business KPIs**
* Analyze **customer behavior using RFM segmentation**
* Evaluate **retention using cohort analysis**
* Build an **interactive BI dashboard for decision-making**

---

## 📊 Key KPIs Defined

| KPI               | Description             | Insight                           |
| ----------------- | ----------------------- | --------------------------------- |
| AOV               | Avg revenue per invoice | Indicates upselling effectiveness |
| CRR               | Customer retention rate | Measures customer loyalty         |
| RCI               | Revenue concentration   | Dependency on top customers       |
| Cancellation Rate | % cancelled invoices    | Revenue leakage indicator         |
| Discount Rate     | Discount impact         | Margin efficiency                 |

---

## 🔍 Deep-Dive Analysis

### 1️⃣ Cohort Retention Analysis

* January cohort shows **strong retention (88.8% Month-1)**
* June cohort achieved **exceptional retention (96.7% Month-5)**
* March & May cohorts show **weak retention (≈28%) → churn risk**

👉 Insight:
Retention varies significantly by acquisition period — **seasonality & sales strategy matter**

---

### 2️⃣ RFM Customer Segmentation

| Segment             | Customers | Strategy                    |
| ------------------- | --------- | --------------------------- |
| Champions           | 278       | Retain & reward             |
| Loyal Customers     | 340       | Upsell & expand             |
| Potential Loyalists | 311       | Nurture                     |
| At Risk             | 240       | Win-back campaigns          |
| Lost                | 50        | Reactivation (low priority) |

👉 Insight:
A small group of customers drives **disproportionate revenue → focus on retention over acquisition**

## 📌 Key Insights

* Revenue is **heavily concentrated** among top customers → risk exposure
* Business is **credit-heavy (69%)** → cash flow concern
* **Cigarettes dominate revenue** → category dependency
* Strong retention cohorts indicate **replicable growth strategy**
* Weak cohorts highlight **customer acquisition quality issues**

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* Focus on **retaining Champion customers**
* Reduce **credit dependency risk**
* Improve **low-performing cohort acquisition strategy**
* Increase **basket size (AOV growth strategies)**
* Monitor **category diversification**
